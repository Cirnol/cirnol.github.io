Art Gallery

This 3D art gallery was made with Unreal Engine 4. It was created for my own educational purposes to learn UE4 and also doubles as a showcase of artwork I've made.

Entrance to the gallery space. Go left for black & white drawings and right for coloful pieces.

Walking up to a canvas will show the title of each artwork, the materials used, and an audio description of each one.

There is a combination of traditional artwork and digital designs throughout the exhibit.
