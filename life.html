Life ReImagined: A Quest

Life ReImagined is a cooperative digital board game to help young people turn climate anxiety into climate action by grounding the game design in the teachings and visions of climate justice movements.
This is an ongoing project created in collaboration with EarthGames and BeTheGreen. I've helped with the art and overall design.

About the Game

Life ReImagined is playable within an internet browser or as a download for PC and Mac. It is best played on a computer.
The game is limited to local play on 1 device and has up to 4 players take turns as they travel across the board and make choices. Each choice will cause a new item to appear in the environment. This is designed to inform players on choices they can take in reality to provide support for just causes and ease eco-anxiety.

The game is still in development with plans to polish current features given a successful fundraising campaign. Click here for more info.

Game length: About 10 minutes.
