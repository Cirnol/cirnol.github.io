About the Game

Caustic Park is fully playable within an internet browser and is best played on a computer.
Equipped with only a flashlight, you must traverse through the halls of an abandoned amusement park while solving light-based puzzles and avoiding the monsters in the dark.

Game Length: About 1 hour.
