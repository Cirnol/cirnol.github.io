SEED

SEED is a puzzle game designed to encourage players to visualize themselves as as agents of positive change in a world on the brink of environmental collapse.
This game was created as a collaborative effort between students at the University of Washington Bothell and surrounding colleges with a focus on promoting diversity in the gaming industry. See more info here.
I helped with 3D modeling and animation using Maya.
